The Problem With Every Other Automation Tool

You spend 6 months building workflows in Zapier, n8n, or Make. Then your auditor asks: "Can a workflow exfiltrate customer data to an external API?" The answer is yes. You fail the audit.

Most automation platforms let workflows call ANY external API. If one workflow gets compromised or a disgruntled employee builds a malicious one, your entire customer database can be uploaded to an external server in seconds.

Deploy secure automation in 2 weeks that passes SOC 2 and ISO 27001 audits on day one. We pre-configured the security controls. Your auditor asks about data exfiltration? We show them the proxy whitelist, network isolation, and complete audit trail. They sign off.
